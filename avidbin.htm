About Bins

 

Bins are used for organising all the video and sound clips for your project. There are three ways of viewing the clips.

 

Firstly as text (by pressing the T icon in the bin) so that file name, duration, in point, outpoint etc. are displayed.

 

Secondly you find the storyboard view (displayed by the S button). This view allows you to write a commentary about each clip and is especially useful when several versions of the shot are captured onto the hard disk.

 

The last option is the frame view. (this is activated by pressing F on the Bin window) It is particularly useful for quickly identifying the clip you want rather than depending on file names which may sometimes confuse you and others.

 

Within this menu are scroll down options indicated by the box with a rectangle with a black line going horizontaly at the centre.
