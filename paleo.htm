This year marked the thirtieth Edition of the Paleo festival de Nyon. It's a music festival that began by the Nyon swimming pool and as it grew in importance migrated into the countryside close to where I live. For a few years it has now been taking place in Asse and many well known artists have come. This year for example Calogero, Rammstein, Jamiroquai, Kyo, Mickey3d and other artists decided to participate.


I have only been to the festival twice but rather than pay for the right to be there I took the option of working as a collaborateur. The collaborateur work for free, payed in drink, food, free stuff and free entrances to the festival. Two years ago I was working inside the camping as part of the trotteur but this year I was working at the accés. You see a huge amount of people entering and leaving the site and some of these people are friendly whilst others aren't. The people you work with are also nice, making those five hour shifts fly by.


Next year I hope to participate again but in the meantime I'll leave you with some pictures from this flickr group.
