Tanzania trip 1999

The Tanzania trip was a project set up by Mr and Mrs Harrison a few years before I went to help Tanzanian school children get a better chance at education. We went to various multintational companies and asked them whether they could give us some money to fund the various projects and earned over 18,000 Chf. In Africa this is a lot of money and we were able to give more students chances at primary and secondary education. I personally find that these forms of projects are very important. We are always led to believe that through replacing a dicator with another leader the people will get a chance at a better life. I believe that we should concentrate on the children themselves. Through providing them with the means to a better education we are giving them more choice for what they will become later on in life. Enjoy these video clips

We set off from Geneva airpor towards Amsterdam and from Amsterdam towards Tanzania. It's an eight hour flight
The street children used to have a long treck to get water using the chariot you see in this video. We built a reservoir for their water

This wooden shack used to be a classroom for over two hundred students. The teacher had to stand outside the window. When the weather was bad the students would suffer from the cold. We actually built a new structure for them to learn in.
