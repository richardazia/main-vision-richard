Conference on schools as agents of change


[ Back ] [ Up ] [ Next ]

During a period four days a group of students from International schools following the IB program joined together in order to discuss the ideas on what a global citizen should have.

These qualities it would hope would encourage understanding thereby reducing the danger of intolerance which usualy leads to friction.

I covered this event by using a number of shots trying to show each part of the event, ending up with 8 hrs.

Firstly there were speakers adressing the students at the event giving the students a background as to what would happen. One teacher for example showed to the students that younger students believe that school is for fun etc. but when asking older kids you get the answer that students are at school to get the IB diploma to get into university... but is this students to become better citizens?

After all these speakers ended there were plenary sessions which consisted of smaller groups of students discussing various ideas to define the terminology for the project and then what the content should be. After this each group got back into the main hall and reported back to the whole assembly so as to show what ideas had come.

After this other plenary sessions took place and discussions progressed and finally a draft of the document was prepared, I do not know how far it has progressed today though.
