2005 Jan

Part of the Enjoyment of having a website is seeing how many people are coming to visit the site each month and observe the progression.I have done one or two pieces of work analysing the website dynamics over a period of months. One of the things that struck me most is the sine wave that is formed representing the earth's rotation over the period of a day. Peaks can be spotted at certain times of the year, when students have the most assignments on the Romans.

It is curious to note that other time Julius Caesar, Slavery and Latin are the terms which appear to be searched for most. Whether this is because people think of these two terms before researching the Romans or not is unsure. What is sure is that this website is popular with a variety of website types linking to it.

Within the last month there have been around nine hundred visitors a day and overall over a quarter of a million visitors from more than a hundred countries.

The purpose and content of the website is changing and it will be interesting to see how the search terms evolve from the Romans towards other subjects.
