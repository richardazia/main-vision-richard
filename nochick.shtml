Surfing the World Wide Waves (WWW)

whilst surfing the world wide web sometimes it's interesting to do some site hoping, going from one domain name to another and seeing what's special about the site. In so doing recently I arrived onto Nochicktrix.com, an interesting forum with some daily rants which are quite amusing. Some include ferrets in trains, people tricked into spending thousands of dollars on the supernatural etc.

The Ambiance within these forums can be quite interesting with the variety of people within. It is quite different from The Flipside in that it's an American site therefore it's more active during an American day rather than say a European day and the ambiance is quite different. Get to know the various people within this community and appreciate exploring various forums and their way of treating the medium.
