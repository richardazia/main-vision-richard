Evaporation and transpiration

Evaporation is different to transpiration as evaporation is water that becomes a gas and transpiration is when it goes through the pores to the outside of leaves.

Infiltration

Infiltration is different to interception, as infiltration is when something passes by something and travels through the cracks.

Surface run off

Surface runs off and through flow are different because one is where the water runs off a surface while the other is through the ground

Flood Hydrograph

A flood hydrograph is a graph to show the amount of water in the river after a storm

Peak rainfall

Peak rainfall is when the most rain is falling from the sky
