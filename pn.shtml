Pacific Nainwak

Games of destruction and hate become quite boring. Pacific nainwak is an amusing game where you are a jardin de nain, a garden gnome whose aim is to seduce the opponent. You have a number of ways by which to do this including various flowers, g-strings, wedding rings and more.

As you become better at flirting you progress through the levels. As you progress you can choose a number of skills you'd like to imrpove, whether charm, witt or dexterity.
