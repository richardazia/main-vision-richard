Introduction:

A lot of time is spent in speaking of the global community and how it means that everyone knows everyone. It is a shame though that when our work is finished in one place we leave and  are unable to share memories any more. Through the use of current compression formats such as windows media files, quicktime movies and real time media it is easier not to lose touch. 

Potential uses:

  • Keeping up with friends and family

  • Informing clients

  • Helping in education

  • Entertainment
