About this site

This site which began in 1996 has been improved over the last three years but there is something missing to it, this being other people's work, it receives many hits and has search engines such as Yahoo, Lycos, Snap, Altavista, the ICQ search engine and many others linking to it, including personal web pages - showing that it is well established online: This could be the reason why some people may wish to contribute to the site in the future.

Why should you contribute?

There are several reasons for which you may wish to contribute to the site: firstly you do not need any experience with web mastering or worry about getting traffic to the site since it is already here, secondly because it's dynamic unlike books you may change the site whenever you want, keep in mind that every morning and evening I check my website so that within a few hours of your sending an e-mail it may be online with full credit to your name and e-mail address...unless you do not wish to have that of course.

Who should contribute?

I am looking for people who are still in school to contribute to my website. This website is designed for younger students, as there are already many technical sites. I am trying to make this a site for teen-agers by teen-agers, taking view points from around the world thereby creating an interesting site for youth.

What you get out of it:

What you get out of contributing to this site is to be part of the information revolution, sharing your knowledge with others and meeting other young people with the same interests.

Contacting me:

If you are interesting in contributing to this site write to richard@main-vision.com and I will answer your e-mail as soon as possible.
