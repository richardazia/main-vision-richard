Who I was at 22

Who am I?

What do I do?

During the weeks since then I have been working on videos for a dance school in Geneva for teaching traditional dancing. Having spent a week at the Telecom 2003 I am waiting for my next work experience. 2003 has been a good year and I'm impatient for 2004
