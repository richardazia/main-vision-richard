The flipside

The Mind is a terrible thing to waste

The Flipside is a british site with an international group of people discussing many different topics. Amongst it's features there is the sharing of images, debates about various topics from everyday chat to science and technology, religion and philosophy, current affairs and more. The group is quite strong and the speed at which conversations take place is fast compared to some other forums.

Hope to add more with time, if you'd like to share your own misfortunes then don't hesitate to contact me
