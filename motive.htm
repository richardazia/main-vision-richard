Richard's statement

As with all topics studied at school we sometimes feel tired in class or have something which we would prefer to talk about. we therefore miss some important important information. I have sometimes felt this and so have decided to create a website about geographical topics which I have studied for the IGCSE for two years. As I learn more while I follow the IB course I will share that knowledge with you.

    Why do I want to share that knowledge with you? Well it's simply that I feel that when you do work for school you are not so much learning for yourself but are learning to take info and reproduce it in a certain way but on this website as you can tell it's a little different. Travel around the website to find out why.
