Le Sentier des Toblerones

When people think of Switzerland they always think of chocolate and cheese. I went on a seventeen kilometer walk along the Sentier des Toblerones in Switzerland. Although you're used to them in chocolate form to me they are a remnant of the second world War. They serve to block and impede the advancing tanks.

Over the years plants, motorways and the railroad have meant that breachs in the Toblerone can be found but this does not make it any less spectacular.

In Brief, Toblerone are not just chocolate.
