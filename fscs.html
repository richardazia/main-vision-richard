Films that are harder to categorise

Film Comments
Requiem for A dream

 An oscar winning film about the problems with any form of drugs. See the parallel between Diet pills and Heroin. Very well edited and quite an interesting film
Clockwork Orange

 Clockwork orange is a very different films from others, mindless violence and more. It's particularly interesting with the accent of the main narrator from London.
Schindler's List Schindler's List directed by Steven Spielberg is based on a real person whom made an effort to save Jews from the gas chambers and extermination camps by getting them to work in his factory. Within this film are some particularly strong images to be seen
Shrek

 Shrek, the tale of an ogre whose peace and quiet is troubled by the new prince. Taking elements from several fairy tales this is an amusing cartoon with the voice talents of Cameron Diaz, Mike Myers and Eddie Murphy
Monsters Inc,

 In the world where Monsters live the way to produce energy is children's screams everything all is not well. A child has come from our world into their world and she must be found.
One flew over the Cukoo's nest

 Jack Nicholson stars in one of his earliest film. He is a convict who has been transfered from being a labour prisoner to being in an asylum to identify whether he is mad or not.
