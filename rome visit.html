Rome is a city I know well. I have been there many times, from when I was younger until now. As a result I see Rome in a different light than first time tourists. I don't rush from monument to monument, instead going from one piazza to another, seeing what the mood is at each one.

I was staying at a youth hostel, finding many Americans, Australians and a variety of other european nationalities coming to visit the city of Rome as well as continuing towards other locations.
